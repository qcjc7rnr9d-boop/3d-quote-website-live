Payment notes

Subscription, usage, and payment processing stay separate.

Trennen standard plans do not add a Trennen checkout fee. Stripe or card processing fees stay separate, and bank transfer has no card processing fee.

Subscription Plan price

Monthly NZD pricing covers the quote allowance for your selected plan.

Usage Extra quotes

Starter is NZ$1 per extra quote. Growth is NZ$0.50. Scale is NZ$0.25.

Payments Processing only

No Trennen checkout fee. Stripe/card processing fees stay separate.

Bank transfer has no card processing fee. Starter and Growth card details are collected by Stripe Checkout, not by Trennen.
