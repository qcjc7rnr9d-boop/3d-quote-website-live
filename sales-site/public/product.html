Features

The parts merchants need to quote online.

Trennen combines the customer quote interface, shop-controlled pricing rules, online payment, and operational tracking.

01

Instant quote widget

Customers upload CAD/STL files, choose material and quantity, and get real-time pricing from your rules. The customer page loads from https://embed.trennen.co.nz/widget.js.

02

Custom domain portal

Serve the quote page from quote.yourdomain.com by pointing a CNAME at quotes.trennen.co.nz.

03

Print process controls

Set up FDM, SLA, SLS, MJF, resin, and custom processes with your own material and machine logic.

04

Multi-part uploads

Keep model groups, notes, quantities, options, shipping, and approval together in the quote path.

05

Payments and checkout

Use Stripe checkout when it helps, while keeping bank transfer and manual approval available.

06

Dashboard tracking

Track quotes, customers, payment state, and job handoff from one merchant dashboard.

Why forms are not enough

3D print quoting needs model and pricing context.

Generic forms collect messages. Trennen collects model files, applies print-shop pricing rules, and gives buyers a clear quote and payment path.

Generic form
  • Collects contact details
  • Leaves file handling and pricing elsewhere
  • Creates more follow-up
Trennen
  • Collects CAD/STL files and quote context
  • Runs shop-controlled pricing rules
  • Connects approval, checkout, and dashboard tracking
