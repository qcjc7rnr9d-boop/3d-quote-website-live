What we collect

We may collect information you submit through this website, including your name, work email, company, quote volume, shop details, selected plan, messages, and signup details.

We may also collect technical information needed to operate and secure the website, such as browser details, request logs, device information, and basic analytics signals.

How we use it

We use website data to respond to enquiries, run signup, send setup links, improve Trennen, prevent abuse, support billing, and keep the website and software reliable.

Signup and setup links

If you start signup, Trennen may use your account and shop details to create a pending or active software account, send a setup link, and keep a record of the plan you selected.

Do not submit someone else's personal information unless you have permission to do so.

Live quote flow preview

The website may load the live Trennen quote widget from the app domain. The widget is intended to show how a buyer quote flow works and may collect technical data needed to load and protect that preview.

Do not upload sensitive or confidential files into a public preview unless you intend to use that preview environment.

Payments and subscriptions

Starter and Growth signup may redirect to Stripe Checkout. Stripe handles card details. Trennen does not receive or store full card numbers through the sales website.

We may store Stripe customer, subscription, payment status, and billing metadata needed to operate subscription billing.

Service providers

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, email delivery, payment processing, security, analytics, and customer support. These providers process data only as needed to provide their services to Trennen.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete personal information where the law gives you that right. Some records may need to be kept for billing, security, accounting, dispute, or legal reasons.

Retention and security

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above. We use practical safeguards to reduce misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, but no internet service can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, email hello@trennen.co.nz.
