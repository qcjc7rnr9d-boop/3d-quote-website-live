The Problem

Manual quoting is killing your margins

Most 3D printing businesses still use spreadsheets or gut-feel for pricing. That's costing you money and customers.

01 / Problem
⏱️

Hours Lost to Calculation

Manually working out material costs, labour rates, and margins for every job is slow, inconsistent, and prone to error. A missed decimal costs you real money.

02 / Problem
💸

Underpricing & Lost Profit

Without a system, you're likely undercharging. Forgetting post-processing time, support removal, or electricity costs eats directly into your profit margins.

03 / Problem
🤝

Slow Responses Lose Deals

Customers expect quotes fast. If you take 2 days to respond, they've already gone to a competitor. Speed is the competitive edge you're missing.

How It Works

Up and running in under 10 minutes

No technical knowledge required. Trennen is designed for people who make things — not software engineers.

01

Set Up Your Rates

Enter your material costs, machine hourly rate, labour cost, and any finishing services. Takes under 10 minutes — no spreadsheet wizardry required.

02

Enter the Job Details

Paste in the print time, select the material and quantity, tick any extras — Trennen does the maths instantly, including your target margin.

03

Send & Win the Job

Hit send. Your client receives a polished, professional quote. Track when they open it and accept — then get printing.
