# Merchant Terms Draft: Platform Operational Access

This is draft merchant-facing wording for the store owner/admin agreement. It should be reviewed and replaced by legal counsel before launch.

## Platform Access

Trennen provides the quoting, customer account, checkout, reporting, and administration software used by participating 3D printing businesses. To operate, secure, support, and troubleshoot the service, authorised Trennen platform administrators may access store configuration, customer records, quote metadata, order records, payment status, audit logs, and related operational data.

Trennen administrators must not access password hashes, reset tokens, raw session data, Stripe secret keys, full card numbers, or payment method details through the platform dashboard. Sensitive platform actions such as viewing detailed order/customer data, impersonating a store, changing Stripe configuration, or suspending/restoring a store should be audit logged.

Stores do not receive a real-time notification for every platform-owner view, but platform access is limited to operational, security, support, billing, and compliance purposes.

## Merchant Responsibility

Each store remains responsible for the accuracy of its customer-facing materials, pricing, shipping labels, support details, terms, privacy wording, fulfilment promises, and any local legal or tax obligations.
