Suggestion helper
How this works

New materials auto-fill from the local library as you type names like PLA, PETG-CF, TPU 95A, AMS TPU, or Nylon PA6. Click Suggest to intentionally refresh populated fields or look up obscure materials online. The system checks common FDM/FFF filament wording first, including PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, Nylon/PA, TPU/TPE, support materials, reinforced filaments, and regional spellings like fibre/fiber. If there is no close match it will offer to look the material up online for obscure or brand-specific materials. Every field remains editable after the suggestion is applied.
