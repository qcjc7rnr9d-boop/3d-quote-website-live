# Takedown, Prohibited Items, And Authority Requests

Use this workflow for unlawful-item concerns, safety concerns, intellectual-property complaints, harmful content, or law-enforcement/regulator requests.

## Intake

- Record requester name, organisation, contact details, date, affected shop, affected customer, order IDs, file names, payment IDs, and the concern raised.
- Preserve relevant order, quote, upload metadata, restricted-item certification, IP/user-agent, email-delivery, payment, and audit records.
- Do not disclose customer data until the request is verified and there is a lawful basis for disclosure.

## Action

- For suspected prohibited or unsafe items, pause fulfilment and notify the participating store.
- For credible infringement or misuse complaints, review the affected upload/order record, preserve evidence, and remove or restrict access where reasonably necessary.
- For law-enforcement or regulator requests, verify identity and authority before disclosure unless emergency circumstances require immediate action.
- Record whether the order was refused, cancelled, refunded, preserved, disclosed, or reopened.

## Platform Position

The checkout certification and terms create customer acknowledgement and enforcement rights. They do not replace legal review, licensing obligations, or the participating store's fulfilment responsibility.
