# Privacy Breach Response

Use this runbook for suspected unauthorised access, loss, disclosure, alteration, deletion, or misuse of personal information held by Trennen or a participating store through the platform.

## Response Steps

1. Record the time the issue was first detected, who reported it, affected systems, and any known affected people.
2. Contain the issue: revoke credentials, disable affected integrations, isolate logs/files, stop further disclosure, and preserve evidence.
3. Assess whether personal information is involved, what categories are affected, whether serious harm is likely, and whether the incident is notifiable.
4. Notify the participating store if store customer data may be involved, and require fast cooperation on customer context and fulfilment impact.
5. If notifiable, notify the Office of the Privacy Commissioner and affected people as soon as practicable. OPC guidance expects notification no later than about 72 hours after becoming aware of a notifiable breach.
6. Keep a written incident timeline, containment record, notifications sent, remediation actions, and follow-up tasks.
